Roles

The team at IDK-5 intentionally did not set out the roles for either project. The logic behind this is that people do not apply for jobs they do not feel comfortable and confident in, therefore why should a student project be any different? As such, tasks were instead assigned during a team meeting where strengths and weaknesses were discussed to ensure the team achieved maximum success. That having been said, there is a common trend in the tasks that were ultimately chosen by each team member and their dream job roles as discussed in their career plans.

Jacob Depares

If a role were to be assigned to Jacob based upon the tasks he’d taken on, it would be that of the project manager. This role is designed to ensure that from planning to closure, the team meets all deadlines whilst maintaining the highest standard of work. In conjunction to this, Jacob would also be responsible of managing the expectations of all stake holding partners and vetting all additional programs used throughout the development and implementation of ComCare.

James Walker

The ComCare project is an elaboration of James Walker’s original Assignment One project. This in conjunction with his strength in website management means that it only makes sense for him to be the user interface architect. His responsibilities include the development of the website structure, timeframe management and quality control. In conjunction to this, he also must liaise with the remainder of the team regarding product testing and content creation.

Larissa Hennessey

Throughout the development of ComCare Larissa has taken on the role of a Project Team Lead or more informally, a Scrum master. This job is designed to co-ordinate the teams and track their sprint progression. In addition to this, Larissa is also responsible for documentation and testing of the program throughout its development as well as designing training measures to be delivered to the end user, so they understand it’s complete capabilities
