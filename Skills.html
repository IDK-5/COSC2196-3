Project lead role

Description

This position at ComCare would be assigned to someone with intention to lead the team for six months following the funding of the project by venture capitalists or particularly the government. This role will require the assigned project lead to oversee and track the progress of all involved employees, create detailed reports for stakeholders, help to eradicate blockers as they appear and ensure that all work is performed to a particular quality. Although this position is in theory assigned to the someone within the pre-existing IDK-5 team, we have listed below the requirements we would expect if we were hiring for this role externally

Skills

  • Functional understanding of entire Microsoft Office Suite
  • 2-4 years’ experience in similar role.
  • Able to communicate professionally.
  • Has the ability to work autonomously or in a team environment.
  • Experience in handset and application development
  • Experience in website and handset application development.
  • Write well-designed, clear and functional coding.
  • Proven experience in leading a team from the design stages to final product release.
  • Ability to clearly communicate progress with stakeholders and answer complex questions in a simple yet concise manner.
  • A dual degree of Business and IT is preferred for this role however other relevant degree holding applicants will be considered.

Software Engineer

Description

The successful applicant for the ComCare Software Engineering position will be responsible for helping to design, structure and code the functional components of the program. This role will be crucial for assisting in the implementation and sprints towards the idea of implementing the regional, state and national stages. This role will be available for 6 months with a possible contract extension depending upon performance.

Skills

  • Functional understanding of entire Microsoft Office Suite.
  • 2-4 years’ experience in similar role.
  • Able to communicate professionally
  • Has the ability to work autonomously or in a team environment.
  • Experience in handset and application development.
  • Experience in website development.
  • Ability to identify and resolve software issues promptly.
  • Write well-designed, clear and functional coding. Able to provide examples of previously executing full software development life cycle.
  • Ability to write in a number of coding languages such as HTML, Java Scripts C, C+, Python
  • A Degree in Information Systems, Computer/Data Sciences or IT is required to be successful in this application.

Data Analyst

Description

As there will be a large amount of data coming in from all users, it’s important to us at ComCare that the applicant for this role is comfortable identifying trends. Ideally the successful applicant would have 2-4 years’ experience in a similar role, be comfortable with team work, communicate concisely, act professionally and able to consistently meet their deadlines or key performance indicators. This role will be available for 6 months with a possible contract extension depending upon performance.

Skills

  • Functional understanding of entire Microsoft Office Suite.
  • 2-4 years’ experience in similar role.
  • Able to communicate professionally.
  • Has the ability to work autonomously or in a team environment.
  • Proven ability to create reports analysing quantitative data which support performances or outcomes.
  • A Degree in Information Systems, Computer/Data Sciences or Business Management would be preferable but is not required if applicant is able to provide evidence proven proficiency in field.
  • Able to identify trends, summarise bulk data and prevent complex information in a simplified yet insightful manner.

IT support analyst

Description

The role of the IT Support Analyst working for ComCare will primarily be based around the idea of a “soft release” of the product. This role would be crucial in identifying any critical issues that a end user would encounter during real-world use and would require the successful applicant to work within the software engineering team to find stable and permanent solutions. This role would continue post-product release as the region of application is widened although may include some job scope shifts. This will be available for 6 months with a possible contract extension depending upon performance.

Skills

  • Function understanding of entire Microsoft Office Suite.
  • 1-3 years’ experience in similar role.
  • Able to communicate professionally.
  • Has the ability to work autonomously or in a team environment.
  • Experience with troubleshooting, issue identifications, lodging of trouble tickets (user request tickets).
  • Experience with handset application development preferable.
  • Ability to identify, diagnose and resolve complex user issues.
  • Whilst tertiary education in some sort of Computer Sciences would be preferable, it is not required should the applicant be able to provide proven proficiency in the field.

ICT infrastructure specialist

Description

It’s important to the team at ComCare that our projects are built upon strong foundations. For that reason, we are seeking a confident ICT Infrastructure Specialist to assist us in putting our best foot forward in security and application design. This role will be available for 6 months with a possible contract extension depending upon performance.

Skills

  • Function understanding of entire Microsoft Office Suite.
  • 2-4 years’ experience in similar role.
  • Able to communicate professionally.
  • Has the ability to work autonomously or in a team environment.
  • Experience in consolidating audit findings into concise reports while still meeting deadlines.
  • Strong experience in Infrastructure Management.
  • Have sound understanding of security implementations and experience in implementing a supporting infrastructure for it within a pre-existing system as well as ability to audit work performed by software engineers to ensure consistent coding and strong system support.
  • Experience in analysing, developing, testing, deploying and maintaining both websites and handset applications.
