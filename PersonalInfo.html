Team Composition

Who is Team #IDK-5?

We started as five individuals with great ideas. However, we had no idea what to call it. We all said, "I don't know.", so we named ourselves the "IDK-5". Shortly, after agreeing on our team name, we were reduced to four people, and now there are three. Focusing on our solutions rather than our name, and with collaborative goals in mind, we agreed to Innovate, Dedicate and expand our Knowledge 'together'. Together we are, IDK-5, committed to everything we do!
