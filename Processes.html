Meeting structure

Companywide meetings will be held once a month to discuss any new initiatives, achievements, blockers and accomplishments. This will ensure that all employees have the same base understanding of the direction of the company. During these meetings, ComCare will be utilising the audience engagement application Slido. This will allow for a question and answer initiative whilst also helping to identify key concerns within the workforce. Due to the number of employees involved, each team would gather in their own meeting room and make a collective MS Teams call. Team meetings will be held of a morning three times a week to discuss progression towards collective goals, discuss blockers and their solutions. It gives the team leaders an opportunity to update their members on policy or process changes. In addition to this, it allows the leader to gauge team morale. A happy workforce is a productive one after all. These gatherings can be performed on site or via MS Teams depending upon the working situation of the team majority that day. One on ones will take place once every second week to discuss individual performance, concerns or accomplishments without an audience. These meetings can be held face to face or over MS Teams.

Progress oversight

During the development of ComCare, progress would be tracked by assigned team leaders who would compile a weekly report for the project lead and project manager to review. It is through this report that sprint progress and blockers can be addressed and tracked to ensure that the project is delivered in time and manpower is redistributed if absolutely necessary.

Excel spreadsheet

In response to the report, all functions of the ComCare service will be broken down into sprints in a spreadsheet and assigned a percentage dependent upon where they are in development. This will allow a convenient and easy to read review which can be sent to any external stakeholders such as government members or supermarkets which have agreed to partake.

Microsoft teams

Each team members situation is different and as such, they may work from home or the office as required. In response to this, ComCare will utilise MS Teams to ensure that all questions can be answered promptly. A team chat will also be created which will allow for brainstorming and quick solutions in the event a team leader cannot be contacted. This is also a convenient way for members to liaise with members from other teams when collaboration is necessary.
